A little bit about ol' me
Born and raised in NY/NJ then moved all around the country. I took the Johnny Cash song "I've Been Everywhere" a little too literally and lived in the scorching hot temps of the desert to breathing the thin air of the Rockies, and then somehow wound up back in NY/NJ.

A big chunk of my recent career has been spent in the world of software development and helping the internet work (with Glitch and Fastly), but I like to think of myself as a "Sean of All Trades" and have dabbled in a lot of different things over the years.

Otherwise I like turtles... I mean sports. Sports and comedy- and apparently decade old references of kid's in zombie face paint. You can catch up on my latest thoughts and stories on my blog (linked below 👇).
